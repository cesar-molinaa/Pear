About Pear

Creating technology that feels natural.

Pear is more than a technology company. We are a team of engineers, designers, and innovators dedicated to creating products that improve the way people work, communicate, and create.

From powerful devices to intelligent software, every Pear product is built around one idea: technology should be simple, elegant, and accessible. By combining cutting-edge engineering with timeless design, we create experiences that seamlessly fit into everyday life.

Today, Pear continues to push the boundaries of innovation while remaining committed to sustainability, privacy, and delivering exceptional experiences for millions of users around the world.

Building a Greener Future

At Pear, sustainability is more than a commitment — it's a responsibility. Every product is designed to reduce environmental impact while delivering the performance and quality our users expect.

Recycled Materials

We maximize the use of recycled aluminum, rare earth elements, and responsibly sourced materials across our devices.

Renewable Energy

Our facilities are powered by renewable energy, reducing emissions while supporting cleaner manufacturing.

Carbon Neutral

Pear is committed to achieving carbon neutrality across our products and operations by 2035.

Our Environmental Goals

  • 100% renewable energy
  • Carbon-neutral operations
  • Recycled aluminum
  • Plastic-free packaging
  • Reduced water consumption
  • Long-lasting, repairable products
